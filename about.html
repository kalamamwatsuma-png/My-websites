Our Story

Born in Taita Taveta,
Built for Kenya

Identity Insight Solution Ltd was founded with a clear conviction: that the biggest barrier to business growth in Kenya's counties is not the lack of willing workers — it's the lack of prepared workers. We set out to close that gap.

Starting in Taita Taveta County, we built a rigorous in-house training framework that takes raw talent and turns it into reliable, professional, deployable capability. What began as a small workforce agency has grown into a multi-service company with over 500 active staff deployed across retail, education, agriculture, mining, and hospitality.

Today, IIS LTD is more than a staffing company — we are a community development engine, a skills incubator, and a strategic partner to businesses and institutions that want to grow with integrity.
