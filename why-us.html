Our Advantage

5 Reasons to Choose IIS LTD

Every reason backed by real results across 7+ years of operations.

01
Trained Before They Arrive

Every person we deploy has gone through our structured in-house training programme — covering job-specific skills, professional conduct, and workplace ethics. You receive staff who are ready to contribute from day one, not people who need weeks of on-the-job orientation.

02
Solutions Built Around You

We don't offer off-the-shelf staffing. We take time to understand your operation, your culture, and your specific needs — then tailor our personnel selection, training content, and support structure to match exactly what you require.

03
Rooted in the Community

We are a Taita Taveta company. We hire and train local youth, which means our workforce understands the local context, language, and culture of your operating environment. This translates directly into better stakeholder relationships and community trust for your business.

04
Ongoing Quality Oversight

Our relationship with you doesn't end at deployment. We maintain active supervision, conduct regular performance reviews, and remain the point of contact for any workforce issues. If a placement isn't working, we act quickly to resolve it — including replacing personnel when needed.

05
Multi-Sector Capability

From schools and retail shops to mining sites and agricultural operations, we have successfully placed and trained personnel across six industries. Our breadth of experience means we understand the distinct demands of each environment and prepare our workforce accordingly.
